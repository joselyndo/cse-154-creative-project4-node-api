Welcome to Garden Walk!

Click "Start" to take you to the path selection screen. There, you can select the amount of flowers you want to encounter on your walk. There'll be three paths with randomized flowers that you can choose to walk down. After choosing a path, you learn a fun fact for each flower you see!
